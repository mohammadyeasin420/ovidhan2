📌 Why Verb Forms Matter

Verbs are the heart of every English sentence. Without a strong understanding of verb forms, you cannot speak, write, or read English correctly. (ক্রিয়াপদ প্রতিটি ইংরেজি বাক্যের হৃদয়। ক্রিয়াপদের রূপ সম্পর্কে ভালো ধারণা না থাকলে আপনি সঠিকভাবে ইংরেজি বলতে, লিখতে বা পড়তে পারবেন না।)

📋 The Three Main Verb Forms

Form Example Bangla Usage
Base Form (V1) go যাওয়া Present tense (I, You, We, They)
Past Form (V2) went গিয়েছিল Past tense
Past Participle (V3) gone গিয়েছে Perfect tenses, Passive voice

📋 Regular vs Irregular Verbs

  • Regular Verbs: Add -ed to form past and past participle (walk → walked → walked). (নিয়মিত ক্রিয়া: past ও past participle-এ -ed যোগ করে।)
  • Irregular Verbs: Change form completely (go → went → gone). (অনিয়মিত ক্রিয়া: সম্পূর্ণ রূপ পরিবর্তিত হয়।)

❌ Common Verb Form Mistakes

Many students make these common mistakes. Avoid them to sound more natural and score higher in exams.

❌ "I have went to school."
✅ "I have gone to school."

📖 Explanation: After "have", use past participle (V3) – "gone" not "went".

❌ "He go to school every day."
✅ "He goes to school every day."

📖 Explanation: Third person singular (he/she/it) needs -s/-es in present tense.

❌ "She was wrote a letter."
✅ "She wrote a letter." / "She was writing a letter."

📖 Explanation: Use V2 for past tense, not V3. Use V4 for continuous tense.

❌ "He can speaks English."
✅ "He can speak English."

📖 Explanation: After modal verbs (can, could, will, etc.), use the base form of the verb.

🧠 Memory Tricks for Verb Forms

  • Group by Pattern: Learn irregular verbs in groups – sing-sang-sung, ring-rang-rung.
  • Mnemonic: For "write-wrote-written" – think "write wrote written" as a rhythm.
  • Bangla Trick: Connect English verbs with similar-sounding Bangla words.
  • Visualization: Picture the verb in action. For "run", imagine someone running.
  • Spaced Repetition: Review verbs regularly using flashcards. Learn 10 new verbs daily and review yesterday's verbs.
💡 Tip: Use the verb in a sentence immediately after learning it. This connects the verb to your real life.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What are the five verb forms in English? ▾
The five verb forms are: Base Form (V1) – go, Past Form (V2) – went, Past Participle (V3) – gone, Present Participle (V4) – going, and 3rd Person Singular (V5) – goes. This page focuses on the three most important forms: Base, Past, and Past Participle.
What is the difference between regular and irregular verbs? ▾
Regular verbs form their past tense by adding -ed (e.g., walk → walked). Irregular verbs change form completely (e.g., go → went → gone). This list includes both regular and irregular verb forms with Bangla meanings.
Why is learning verb forms important for BCS and IELTS? ▾
Verb forms are essential for BCS, IELTS, and Bank Job exams. Questions appear in error correction, sentence completion, and transformation sections. Mastering verb forms helps you score higher and avoid common mistakes.
How can I remember irregular verb forms? ▾
