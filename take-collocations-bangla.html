📖 Introduction: What Are "Take" Collocations?

"Take" is a very versatile verb in English. We use it for transportation, actions, time, and many daily activities. ("Take" ইংরেজির একটি বহুমুখী ক্রিয়াপদ। আমরা এটি পরিবহন, কাজ, সময় এবং অনেক দৈনন্দিন কার্যকলাপের জন্য ব্যবহার করি।)

However, "take" doesn't go with every word. For example, we say "take a shower" – not "do a shower". We say "take a break" – not "make a break". (তবে "take" প্রতিটি শব্দের সাথে যায় না। উদাহরণস্বরূপ, আমরা "take a shower" বলি – "do a shower" নয়।)

📝 "I need to take a shower after the gym." – Sounds natural, right?
📝 "আমার জিমের পরে গোসল করা দরকার।" – স্বাভাবিক শোনায়, তাই না?
❌ "I need to do a shower after the gym." – Now it sounds wrong!
❌ "আমার জিমের পরে গোসল করা দরকার।" – এখন এটি ভুল শোনায়!

In this guide, I'll walk you through 40+ "take" collocations organised by meaning, with Bangla translations and example sentences. Let's get started! (এই গাইডে, আমি আপনাকে ৪০+ "take" কলোকেশন শেখাবো, বাংলা অর্থ এবং উদাহরণ সহ। শুরু করা যাক!)

⚡ Take vs Get – The Key Difference

This is one of the most common questions students ask. Here's the simple rule: (এটি শিক্ষার্থীদের সবচেয়ে সাধারণ প্রশ্নগুলির মধ্যে একটি। এখানে সহজ নিয়মটি রয়েছে:)

TAKE = receive, accept, or perform an action
TAKE = গ্রহণ, মেনে নেওয়া বা একটি কাজ সম্পাদন
Examples: take a bus, take a shower, take a risk
উদাহরণ: take a bus, take a shower, take a risk

GET = obtain, receive, or become
GET = প্রাপ্ত করা, গ্রহণ করা বা হওয়া
Examples: get a job, get married, get better
উদাহরণ: get a job, get married, get better
💡 Quick Memory Trick:
💡 দ্রুত মনে রাখার টিপ:
Take = you do an action (take a shower, take a photo)
Take = আপনি একটি কাজ করেন (take a shower, take a photo)
Get = you obtain or become (get a job, get tired)
Get = আপনি প্রাপ্ত করেন বা হন (get a job, get tired)

❌ Common Mistakes with "Take" Collocations

Many students make these common mistakes with "take". Let's look at them so you can avoid them. (অনেক শিক্ষার্থী "take" দিয়ে এই সাধারণ ভুলগুলি করে। আসুন সেগুলি দেখি যাতে আপনি সেগুলি এড়াতে পারেন।)

❌ "I need to do a shower."
✅ "I need to take a shower."

📖 Explanation: "Shower" uses "take", not "do".

❌ "She made a break."
✅ "She took a break."

📖 Explanation: "Break" uses "take", not "make".

❌ "I need to get a photo."
✅ "I need to take a photo."

📖 Explanation: "Photo" uses "take", not "get".

❌ "He took a risk." (correct) – this one is correct, but many confuse with "make a risk" – we only say "take a risk".
✅ "He took a risk."

📖 Explanation: Always "take a risk", never "make a risk".

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the meaning of "take collocations"? ▾
Take collocations are combinations of words where the verb "take" is used with a noun or phrase to create a common expression. For example, "take a shower" means to have a shower. These collocations are essential for speaking English naturally.
Why are "take" collocations important for BCS and IELTS? ▾
Take collocations are frequently tested in BCS, IELTS, and Bank Job exams. Questions appear in error correction, sentence completion, and vocabulary sections. Mastering them can help you score higher.
What is the difference between "take" and "get" collocations? ▾
Take collocations often involve transportation (take a bus), actions (take a shower), or time (take a break). Get collocations often involve obtaining or receiving something (get a job, get married).
