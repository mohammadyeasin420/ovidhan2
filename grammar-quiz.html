📚 Why Grammar Matters for BCS, IELTS & Bank Jobs

Grammar is the foundation of English. BCS, IELTS, Bank Jobs, HSC, and university admission exams all test your grammar knowledge. (ব্যাকরণ ইংরেজির ভিত্তি। BCS, IELTS, ব্যাংক জব, HSC এবং বিশ্ববিদ্যালয় ভর্তি পরীক্ষা সবগুলোই ব্যাকরণ পরীক্ষা করে।)

📌 Topics Covered in This Quiz

  • Subject-Verb Agreement: "Everyone is" vs "Everyone are" – make sure your subject and verb match.
  • Tenses: Present, Past, Future – Simple, Continuous, Perfect, Perfect Continuous.
  • Articles: "a", "an", "the" – when to use them and when not to.
  • Prepositions: "in", "on", "at", "to", "for" – showing relationships between words.
  • Mixed Grammar: Comparative forms, passive voice, narration, and common mistakes.

📌 Common Grammar Mistakes to Avoid

❌ "She don't know the answer."
✅ "She doesn't know the answer."

📖 Explanation: "She" is third person singular → use "doesn't" not "don't".

❌ "He is senior than me."
✅ "He is senior to me."

📖 Explanation: "Senior" is followed by "to", not "than".

❌ "I have went there."
✅ "I have gone there."

📖 Explanation: After "have", use past participle (V3) – "gone" not "went".

🎯 Tips for BCS & IELTS Grammar Questions

  • Read the full sentence before choosing an answer – context matters.
  • Watch for subject-verb agreement – always check if the subject is singular or plural.
  • Learn common collocations – e.g., "good at", "interested in", "afraid of".
  • Practice regularly – the more you practice, the faster you'll identify correct answers.
