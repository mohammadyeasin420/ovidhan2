📚 What Are the Parts of Speech?

Every word in English belongs to one of eight categories called parts of speech. Understanding these categories is essential for BCS, IELTS, Bank Jobs, HSC, and university admission. (ইংরেজির প্রতিটি শব্দ আটটি শ্রেণির একটিতে পড়ে, যাকে Parts of Speech বলে।)

1. Noun (বিশেষ্য)

A noun names a person, place, thing, or idea. For example, Rahim, Dhaka, book, happiness. Learn more in our Noun Rules guide.

📝 Rahim lives in Dhaka.
রহিম ঢাকায় বাস করে।
Bangladesh won the match.
বাংলাদেশ ম্যাচ জিতেছে।

2. Pronoun (সর্বনাম)

A pronoun replaces a noun to avoid repetition. Examples: he, she, it, they, we, I. Our Pronoun Rules guide has more details.

📝 He and I went to the market.
সে এবং আমি বাজারে গিয়েছিলাম।

3. Verb (ক্রিয়া)

A verb describes an action, occurrence, or state of being. Examples: run, eat, is, become, have. Visit our Verb Rules guide.

📝 She speaks English fluently.
সে ইংরেজি সাবলীলভাবে কথা বলে।

4. Adjective (বিশেষণ)

An adjective describes or modifies a noun or pronoun. Examples: beautiful, tall, happy, red. Our Adjective Rules guide covers this in depth.

📝 The beautiful flower bloomed.
সুন্দর ফুলটি ফুটেছে।

5. Adverb (ক্রিয়াবিশেষণ)

An adverb modifies a verb, adjective, or another adverb. Examples: quickly, very, always, well. Learn more in our Adverb Rules guide.

📝 He runs quickly.
সে দ্রুত দৌড়ায়।

6. Preposition (পদান্বয়ী অব্যয়)

A preposition shows the relationship between a noun/pronoun and another word. Examples: in, on, at, to, for, with, by. Our Preposition Rules guide is essential for exams.

📝 She placed the book on the table.
সে বইটি টেবিলে রাখল।

7. Conjunction (সংযোজক অব্যয়)

A conjunction connects words, phrases, or clauses. Examples: and, but, or, because, although. For more, see our Conjunction Rules guide.

📝 I wanted to go, but I was tired.
আমি যেতে চেয়েছিলাম, কিন্তু আমি ক্লান্ত ছিলাম।

8. Interjection (আবেগসূচক অব্যয়)

An interjection expresses strong emotion. Examples: Wow! Oh! Ouch! Hurrah!. Our Interjection Rules guide explains more.

📝 Wow! That is amazing.
বাহ! এটা আশ্চর্যজনক।

❌ Common Mistakes

❌ "She is a beauty girl."
✅ "She is a beautiful girl."

📖 Explanation: "Beauty" is a noun. "Beautiful" is the adjective that describes the girl.

❌ "He runs quick."
✅ "He runs quickly."

📖 Explanation: "Quick" is an adjective. "Quickly" is the adverb that modifies the verb "runs".

🧠 Quick Tip

💡 Remember: When analyzing a sentence, ask yourself:
• Is it a person, place, thing, or idea? → Noun
• Does it replace a noun? → Pronoun
• Does it describe a noun? → Adjective
• Does it describe a verb, adjective, or adverb? → Adverb
• Does it show action or state? → Verb
• Does it show relationship? → Preposition
• Does it connect words? → Conjunction
• Does it express emotion? → Interjection
