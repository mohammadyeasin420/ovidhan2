📌 Master English Idioms with Bangla Meanings & Examples

Idioms are expressions that don't mean exactly what the words say. They add colour and depth to your English – and they are essential for BCS, IELTS, Bank Jobs, and daily conversation. This dictionary contains 200+ commonly used idioms with their Bangla meanings and example sentences. 💡 Tip: Try to use at least one new idiom every day in your writing or speaking – it will make your English sound more natural and confident!
