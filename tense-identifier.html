📚 Tense Overview – 12 Tenses

English has 12 tenses divided into Present, Past, and Future with Indefinite, Continuous, Perfect, and Perfect Continuous forms. (ইংরেজিতে মোট ১২টি টেন্স রয়েছে – Present, Past, Future এবং Indefinite, Continuous, Perfect, Perfect Continuous।)

📌 Present Tenses

TenseStructureExample
Present IndefiniteSubject + V1 (s/es)I read books.
Present ContinuousSubject + am/is/are + V-ingI am reading a book.
Present PerfectSubject + have/has + V3I have read this book.
Present Perfect ContinuousSubject + have/has + been + V-ingI have been reading for 2 hours.

📌 Past Tenses

TenseStructureExample
Past IndefiniteSubject + V2I read a book yesterday.
Past ContinuousSubject + was/were + V-ingI was reading a book.
Past PerfectSubject + had + V3I had read the book.
Past Perfect ContinuousSubject + had + been + V-ingI had been reading for 2 hours.

📌 Future Tenses

TenseStructureExample
Future IndefiniteSubject + will + V1I will read a book.
Future ContinuousSubject + will be + V-ingI will be reading a book.
Future PerfectSubject + will have + V3I will have read the book.
Future Perfect ContinuousSubject + will have been + V-ingI will have been reading for 2 hours.
