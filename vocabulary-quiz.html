📚 Why a Strong Vocabulary Matters

A rich vocabulary is the foundation of English fluency. Whether you're preparing for BCS, IELTS, Bank Jobs, HSC, or just improving your daily communication, knowing the right words – and their Bangla meanings – gives you confidence and clarity. (একটি শক্তিশালী ভোকাবুলারি ইংরেজি সাবলীলতার ভিত্তি।)

📌 Tips to Expand Your Vocabulary

  • Learn in groups – study synonyms and antonyms together (e.g., happy, joyful, cheerful vs sad, sorrowful).
  • Use new words daily – try to write sentences with them. Practice makes permanent.
  • Read regularly – read newspapers, novels, and articles in English. Notice how words are used.
  • Quiz yourself – regular testing helps you remember. Our Vocabulary Quiz is perfect for that.
  • Connect with Bangla – understanding the Bengali meaning makes it easier to recall the English word.

📌 Common Vocabulary Mistakes

❌ "I am very affectionate to him."
✅ "I am very affectionate towards him."

📖 Explanation: “Affectionate” is followed by “towards”.

❌ "She is averse to hard work."
✅ "She is averse to hard work."

📖 Explanation: “Averse” takes the preposition “to”.
