📖 Why Bank Job Vocabulary Matters

The English section in Bangladesh Bank job exams is a decisive factor for thousands of candidates every year. Bank job exams – including Bangladesh Bank, Sonali Bank, Janata Bank, Agrani Bank, and other commercial banks – heavily test vocabulary, synonyms, antonyms, idioms, and reading comprehension. In this comprehensive guide, we have compiled 200+ of the most frequently tested English words from previous bank job exams. Each word includes Bangla meaning, synonyms, antonyms, and example sentences to help you master them quickly.

❌ Common Vocabulary Mistakes in Bank Exams

Many candidates lose marks due to these common mistakes. Avoid them to score higher.

❌ Mixing up "Affect" and "Effect"
✅ "Affect" = verb (to influence), "Effect" = noun (a result)

📖 Explanation: "The new policy will affect the economy." (verb) / "The effect was immediate." (noun)

❌ Confusing "Liable" and "Libel"
✅ "Liable" = legally responsible, "Libel" = defamation in writing

📖 Explanation: "The bank is liable for the loss." / "He sued for libel."

❌ Using "Less" instead of "Fewer"
✅ "Fewer" = countable items, "Less" = uncountable items

📖 Explanation: "Fewer customers" (countable) / "Less money" (uncountable)

📌 3. Practice Grammar and Vocabulary Together

Revise one grammar topic each day while simultaneously learning new vocabulary words. This integrated approach improves overall English proficiency.

Practice with 10,000+ MCQ questions to simulate exam conditions. Regular testing helps identify weak areas and builds confidence.

📌 5. Common Bank Exam Vocabulary Question Types

  • One-word Substitutions: Replace a phrase with a single word.
  • Idioms and Phrases: Understand the meaning of common expressions.
  • Sentence Completion: Fill in blanks with appropriate vocabulary.

🧠 Memory Tricks for Bank Job Vocabulary

  • Group by Theme: Learn banking words together (Account, Accrue, Amortize, etc.) and finance words together (Capital, Income, Revenue, etc.).
  • Mnemonic: For "Accrue" – think "a + crew" – the crew accumulates over time.
  • Bangla Trick: Connect "Collateral" with "জামানত" – think of "college" as something that guarantees your future.
  • Visualization: Picture the word in action. For "Withdrawal", imagine taking money out of an ATM.
  • Spaced Repetition: Review words regularly using flashcards. Learn 10 new words daily and review yesterday's words.
💡 Tip: Use the word in a sentence immediately after learning it. This connects the word to your real life.
টিপ: শিখার সাথে সাথে শব্দটি একটি বাক্যে ব্যবহার করুন। এটি শব্দটিকে আপনার বাস্তব জীবনের সাথে সংযুক্ত করে।
