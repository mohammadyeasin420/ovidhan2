📌 Master English Phrasal Verbs with Bangla Meanings & Examples

Phrasal verbs are combinations of a verb and a particle (preposition or adverb) that create a new meaning. They are essential for natural English conversation, BCS, IELTS, and Bank Jobs. This dictionary contains 200+ common phrasal verbs with their Bangla meanings and example sentences. 💡 Tip: Don't try to memorize them all at once – focus on the most common ones and practise them in context. Notice how the particle changes the meaning of the main verb.
