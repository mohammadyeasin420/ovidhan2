📚 Why Grammar Patterns Matter

Knowing the correct grammar patterns is essential for BCS, IELTS, Bank Jobs, HSC, and university admission. Using the wrong preposition or pattern can change the meaning of your sentence or make it grammatically incorrect. 📌 Common Pattern Mistakes

❌ "I am interested to learn English."
✅ "I am interested in learning English."

📖 Explanation: Use "interested in" with a gerund (-ing form). Learn more →

❌ "She is afraid from spiders."
✅ "She is afraid of spiders."

📖 Explanation: Use "afraid of" when expressing fear. Learn more →

❌ "She is married with a doctor."
✅ "She is married to a doctor."

📖 Explanation: Use "married to" when referring to a person. Learn more →
