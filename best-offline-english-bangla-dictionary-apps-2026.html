Frequently Asked Questions

Which is the best offline dictionary app for BCS in 2026?
The best offline dictionary app for BCS in 2026 is Ovidhan. It has a BCS Special Category, daily Mock Tests, Spaced Repetition flashcards, and Weakness Analysis – everything you need for BCS English preparation in one app.
Does Ovidhan really work 100% offline?
Yes – Ovidhan is 100% offline. All 12,658+ words, Mock Tests, Flashcards, and Gamification are stored on your device. Internet is only required for AdMob ads – but if there's no internet, the app doesn't stop working.
Can I use Google Translate as a dictionary?
Google Translate is a translation tool – not a dictionary. It translates sentences and paragraphs, but you won't find synonyms, antonyms, idioms, or phrasal verbs needed for BCS or Bank Job preparation. A dedicated dictionary app is much more useful for exams.
Is Ovidhan completely free?
Yes – all core features of Ovidhan are free: Dictionary, Mock Test, Flashcards, Gamification, and all categories. The app shows some non-intrusive ads. A premium tier will come in the future to remove ads and add advanced features, but the main learning features will always be free.
Is there an iOS version of Ovidhan?
Currently, Ovidhan is available only on Android (Google Play Store). There are plans for an iOS version, but over 95% of smartphone users in Bangladesh use Android, so it's not a major issue for most users.
