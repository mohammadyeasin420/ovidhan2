❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is an email generator? ▾
An email generator is a tool that helps you quickly create professional email drafts. You select a situation (like a business inquiry or follow-up), provide your details, and the tool generates a complete email with a subject line, body, and signature.
Is this email generator free? ▾
Yes. All Ovidhan tools are completely free for Bangladeshi students and professionals.
What types of emails can I generate? ▾
You can generate business inquiry emails, follow-up emails, complaint emails, job application emails, meeting request emails, thank you emails, and leave application emails.
Can I customize the generated email? ▾
Yes. Once the email is generated, you can edit it directly in the text area before copying it. You can also adjust the tone between formal, semi-formal, and informal.
