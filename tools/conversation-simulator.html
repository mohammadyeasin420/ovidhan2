❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is a conversation simulator? ▾
A conversation simulator is an interactive tool that helps you practice English speaking in real-life scenarios. You choose a situation like an airport, restaurant, or office, and the tool guides you through a natural conversation with multiple-choice responses.
Is this conversation simulator free? ▾
Yes. All Ovidhan tools are completely free for Bangladeshi students and learners.
What scenarios are available? ▾
You can practice conversations for Restaurant, Office, Airport, Shopping, Hospital, and Job Interview scenarios. Each scenario has 5-7 natural conversation turns.
Can I use this for IELTS preparation? ▾
Yes. The conversation simulator is excellent for IELTS Speaking practice. It helps you build confidence and fluency in real conversations.
How does the scoring work? ▾
You earn points for choosing correct and natural responses. The score reflects your confidence in handling real conversations. A higher score means more natural and accurate English.
