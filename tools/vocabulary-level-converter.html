❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the CEFR level of a word? ▾
CEFR (Common European Framework of Reference) levels range from A1 (Beginner) to C2 (Mastery). Each word has a difficulty level that indicates how advanced it is. This tool tells you the CEFR level of any English word.
Is this tool free? ▾
Yes. All Ovidhan tools are completely free for Bangladeshi students and learners.
How can I use this for IELTS/BCS preparation? ▾
By knowing the level of each word, you can focus on learning vocabulary appropriate for your exam. For IELTS, aim for B2-C1 words. For BCS, both B1 and B2 are common.
Can I find synonyms and antonyms? ▾
Yes. For most words, we provide synonyms, antonyms, example sentences, and pronunciation to help you fully understand and use the word.
