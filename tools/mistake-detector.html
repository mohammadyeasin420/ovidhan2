❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is a mistake detector? ▾
A mistake detector is a tool that finds and fixes common English errors in real-time as you type. It highlights mistakes, provides corrections, and explains why they are wrong.
Is this tool free? ▾
Yes. All Ovidhan tools are completely free for Bangladeshi students and learners.
What types of mistakes does it detect? ▾
It detects subject-verb agreement errors, article errors, preposition mistakes, verb form errors, word order issues, and common Bangladeshi mistakes like 'I am agree' and 'I am knowing.'
Can I use this for IELTS/BCS preparation? ▾
Yes. This tool is excellent for IELTS and BCS preparation. It helps you identify and fix common grammar errors that examiners look for.
