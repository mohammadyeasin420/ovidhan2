❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is a collocation builder? ▾
A collocation builder is a tool that shows you natural word pairs in English. For example, 'make a decision,' 'heavy rain,' or 'strong coffee.' Learning collocations helps you sound more natural and fluent.
Is this tool free? ▾
Yes. All Ovidhan tools are completely free for Bangladeshi students and learners.
Why are collocations important for IELTS? ▾
IELTS examiners look for natural word combinations. Using collocations correctly shows higher language proficiency and can significantly improve your band score in writing and speaking.
Can I save my searches? ▾
Yes. Your recent searches are saved automatically in your browser using localStorage. You can clear them anytime.
