❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is a phrase builder? ▾
A phrase builder is a tool that helps you learn common English phrases and collocations. You type a verb, and the tool shows you 10+ phrases with Bangla translations and example sentences.
Is this phrase builder free? ▾
Yes. All Ovidhan tools are completely free for Bangladeshi students and learners.
What are collocations? ▾
Collocations are words that naturally go together in English. For example, 'take a break' is a collocation. Learning collocations helps you sound more natural and fluent.
Can I save my searches? ▾
Yes. Your recent searches are saved automatically in your browser using localStorage. You can clear them anytime.
