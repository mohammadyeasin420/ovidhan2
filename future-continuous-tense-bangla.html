📖 1. Introduction

Future Continuous Tense (ভবিষ্যৎ নিরন্তর কাল) is used to describe actions that will be ongoing at a specific time in the future. (Future Continuous Tense ভবিষ্যতে কোনো নির্দিষ্ট সময়ে চলতে থাকবে এমন কাজ বোঝাতে ব্যবহৃত হয়।)

For Bangladeshi students, this tense is often confused with Future Simple. The key difference is: Future Simple describes a single action (I will go), while Future Continuous describes an ongoing action (I will be going at 8 PM). (বাংলাদেশী শিক্ষার্থীদের জন্য এই টেন্সটি প্রায়ই Future Simple-এর সাথে বিভ্রান্ত হয়। মূল পার্থক্য: Future Simple একটি একক কাজ বোঝায়, Future Continuous একটি চলমান কাজ বোঝায়।)

💡 Remember This

Future Continuous = Ongoing action + Specific future time

বাংলা: ভবিষ্যতে চলতে থাকা কাজ + নির্দিষ্ট সময়

📝 "I will be reading at 8 PM tomorrow."
📝 "আমি আগামীকাল রাত ৮টায় পড়তে থাকব।"
📝 "She will be working when you arrive."
📝 "তুমি পৌঁছানোর সময় সে কাজ করতে থাকবে।"

📚 2. Definition & Usage

Future Continuous Tense is used to describe:

  • Actions that will be ongoing at a specific future time.
    (ভবিষ্যতে কোনো নির্দিষ্ট সময়ে চলতে থাকা কাজ।)
    Example: "I will be studying at 9 PM tonight."
  • Actions that will be interrupted by another future action.
    (অন্য কোনো ভবিষ্যত কাজ দ্বারা বাধাপ্রাপ্ত হবে এমন কাজ।)
    Example: "I will be sleeping when you call."
  • Actions that will happen as a result of a routine or arrangement.
    (রুটিন বা ব্যবস্থার ফল হিসেবে ঘটবে এমন কাজ।)
    Example: "I will be going to the gym tomorrow morning."
  • Actions that will be happening simultaneously.
    (একই সময়ে চলতে থাকা কাজ।)
    Example: "While I will be cooking, he will be reading."
👨‍🏫 Teacher Tip

Students remember this easily: Specific time + Ongoing = Future Continuous

📐 3. Structure – Affirmative, Negative & Interrogative

🔹 Affirmative (হ্যাঁ বোধক)

Subject + will be + Verb(ing)
SubjectWill beV-ingExample
Iwill bereadingI will be reading at 8 PM.
Youwill bestudyingYou will be studying tomorrow.
Hewill beworkingHe will be working when you come.
Shewill bewritingShe will be writing a letter.
Itwill berainingIt will be raining tomorrow.
Wewill beplayingWe will be playing cricket.
Theywill bewaitingThey will be waiting for you.

🔸 Negative (না বোধক)

Subject + will not be + Verb(ing)
SubjectWill notBeV-ingExample
Iwill notbereadingI will not be reading at 8 PM.
Hewill notbeworkingHe will not be working when you come.
Theywill notbewaitingThey will not be waiting for you.

Short form: won't = will not

🔹 Interrogative (প্রশ্ন বোধক)

Will + Subject + be + Verb(ing)?
WillSubjectBeV-ingExample
WillyoubereadingWill you be reading at 8 PM?
WillhebeworkingWill he be working when I come?
WilltheybewaitingWill they be waiting for us?

⚡ 5. Mini Comparison – Future Continuous vs Future Simple

This is a common confusion. Here's the clear difference: (এটি একটি সাধারণ বিভ্রান্তি। এখানে স্পষ্ট পার্থক্য রয়েছে:)

FeatureFuture SimpleFuture Continuous
Usage Single future action Ongoing future action
Structure will + V1 will be + V-ing
Example I will go tomorrow. I will be going at 8 PM.
Bangla আমি আগামীকাল যাব। আমি রাত ৮টায় যেতে থাকব।
Time Expression tomorrow, next week at 8 PM, this time tomorrow
💡 Quick Memory Trick:
Single action → Future Simple  |  Ongoing action → Future Continuous
একক কাজ → Future Simple  |  চলমান কাজ → Future Continuous

📋 6. Rules with Examples

Rule 1: Ongoing Action at a Specific Future Time

✅ "I will be studying at 9 PM tonight."
✅ "আমি আজ রাত ৯টায় পড়তে থাকব।"
✅ "She will be working at this time tomorrow."
✅ "সে আগামীকাল এই সময় কাজ করতে থাকবে।"

Rule 2: Interrupted Future Action

✅ "I will be sleeping when you call me."
✅ "তুমি ফোন করার সময় আমি ঘুমাতে থাকব।"
✅ "He will be eating when we arrive."
✅ "আমরা পৌঁছানোর সময় সে খেতে থাকবে।"

Rule 3: Routine or Arrangement

✅ "I will be going to the gym tomorrow morning."
✅ "আমি আগামীকাল সকালে জিমে যেতে থাকব।"

Rule 4: Simultaneous Actions

✅ "While I will be cooking, he will be reading."
✅ "আমি রান্না করতে থাকব, আর সে পড়তে থাকবে।"

❌ 7. Common Mistakes – Bangladeshi Students

❌ "I will be go to Dhaka tomorrow."
✅ "I will be going to Dhaka tomorrow."

📖 After "will be", always use the -ing form.

❌ "I will be study at 8 PM."
✅ "I will be studying at 8 PM."

📖 Always use the -ing form after "will be".

❌ "I will be here tomorrow."
✅ "I will be here tomorrow." OR "I will be staying here tomorrow."

📖 "Will be" alone is Future Simple. For Future Continuous, add the -ing form.

💬 10. Real Life Examples

See how Future Continuous is used in everyday situations. (দৈনন্দিন পরিস্থিতিতে Future Continuous কীভাবে ব্যবহৃত হয় তা দেখুন।)

🏫 At School
"I will be studying for my exam at 9 PM."
"আমি রাত ৯টায় আমার পরীক্ষার জন্য পড়তে থাকব।"
"The teacher will be teaching when you arrive."
"তুমি পৌঁছানোর সময় শিক্ষক শেখাতে থাকবেন।"
🏢 At Office
"I will be working on this project all day tomorrow."
"আমি আগামীকাল সারাদিন এই প্রকল্পে কাজ করতে থাকব।"
"She will be attending a meeting at 10 AM."
"সে সকাল ১০টায় একটি মিটিংয়ে অংশগ্রহণ করতে থাকবে।"
🏠 Daily Conversation
"I will be cooking dinner when you arrive."
"তুমি পৌঁছানোর সময় আমি রাতের খাবার রান্না করতে থাকব।"
"We will be watching a movie at 8 PM."
"আমরা রাত ৮টায় একটি সিনেমা দেখতে থাকব।"

🚫 11. When NOT to Use Future Continuous

Future Continuous is not used for: (Future Continuous ব্যবহার করা হয় না:)

  • Single future actions – Use Future Simple instead.
  • Stative verbs (know, believe, love, etc.) – Use Future Simple instead.
  • Decisions made at the moment of speaking – Use Future Simple instead.
❌ "I will be go to Dhaka tomorrow."
✅ "I will go to Dhaka tomorrow."

📖 A single future action → Future Simple.

❌ "I will be knowing the answer tomorrow."
✅ "I will know the answer tomorrow."

📖 "Know" is a stative verb → use Future Simple.

👨‍🏫 14. Teacher Tips

💡 Tip 1 – Specific Time Expressions

At this time tomorrow, at 8 PM, this time next week – these words are your clues for Future Continuous!

💡 Tip 2 – Interrupted Actions

When + interrupting action (Future Simple) | While + ongoing action (Future Continuous)

💡 Tip 3 – Stative Verbs

Know, believe, love, hate, want – these are NEVER used in continuous form. Use Future Simple instead.

❓ 18. Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Future Continuous Tense in Bangla? ▾
Future Continuous Tense (ভবিষ্যৎ নিরন্তর কাল) is used to describe actions that will be ongoing at a specific time in the future. For example: 'I will be reading at 8 PM tomorrow' – আমি আগামীকাল রাত ৮টায় পড়তে থাকব।
What is the structure of Future Continuous Tense? ▾
The structure is: Subject + will be + Verb(ing). For example: I will be reading, He will be studying, They will be playing. Negative: Subject + will not be + V-ing. Interrogative: Will + Subject + be + V-ing?
What is the difference between Future Continuous and Future Simple? ▾
Future Simple is used for a future action (I will go). Future Continuous is used for an ongoing future action (I will be going at 8 PM). The continuous form emphasizes the duration or ongoing nature.
How do we form negative sentences in Future Continuous? ▾
We use 'will not be' (won't be) + the -ing form. For example: 'I will not be reading' or 'I won't be reading.'
What are the common signal words for Future Continuous? ▾
Common signal words are: at this time tomorrow, at 8 PM tomorrow, this time next week, all day tomorrow, when you come, while.
Why is Future Continuous important for BCS and IELTS? ▾
Future Continuous is frequently tested in BCS, IELTS, and Bank Job exams. Questions appear in error correction, sentence completion, and transformation. The use of 'will be' and '-ing' ending are especially important.

📝 19. Summary

Future Continuous Tense is used to describe actions that will be ongoing at a specific future time. Here are the key takeaways:

  • Structure: Subject + will be + V-ing
  • Negative: Subject + will not be + V-ing
  • Interrogative: Will + Subject + be + V-ing?
  • Usage: Ongoing at specific time, interrupted actions, simultaneous actions, routines
  • Signal Words: at this time tomorrow, at 8 PM, this time next week, when, while
  • Avoid: Using stative verbs (know, believe, etc.) in continuous form
  • Key Difference: Future Simple = single action | Future Continuous = ongoing action
