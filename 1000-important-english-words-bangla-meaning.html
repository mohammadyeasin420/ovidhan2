How to Study These 1000 Words Effectively

Just reading the list once is not enough. To really remember these words, you should follow a simple plan:

  1. Learn 20–30 words per day instead of trying to memorize everything at once.
  2. Write your own example sentences in a notebook using each word.
  3. Review old words regularly using spaced repetition (Ovidhan app has this built-in).
  4. Use the words in speaking with friends, teachers, or in online chats.
  5. Practice mock tests to see which words you still forget.
