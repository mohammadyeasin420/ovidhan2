❌ Common Vocabulary Mistakes

Many students make these common mistakes. Avoid them to sound more natural and score higher in exams.

❌ "I am agree with you."
✅ "I agree with you."

📖 Explanation: "Agree" is a verb – we don't use "am" with it. Just say "I agree".

❌ "She is very beautifully."
✅ "She is very beautiful."

📖 Explanation: After "is", we use an adjective ("beautiful"), not an adverb ("beautifully").

❌ "I have a important meeting."
✅ "I have an important meeting."

📖 Explanation: "Important" starts with a vowel sound → use "an".

🎯 Exam Tips – BCS, IELTS, Bank Jobs & University Admission

📘 For BCS & Bank Jobs

  • Focus on synonyms and antonyms – they ask at least 5-10 questions on these.
  • Learn word families (e.g., importance, important, importantly, unimportant).
  • Pay attention to collocations (e.g., make a decision, not do a decision).
  • Study the most frequently asked BCS words from previous years.

🌍 For IELTS

  • Use a variety of vocabulary in your writing and speaking tasks.
  • Learn words in context – don't just memorize lists.
  • Practice using synonyms to avoid repetition.
  • Focus on academic words (AWL) for Writing Task 2.

🎒 For University Admission

  • Master the first 500 words from this list – they appear most frequently.
  • Practice writing sentences with each word.
  • Review the Bangla meanings to ensure you understand fully.

🧠 Memory Tricks to Remember 1000 Important Words

  • Mnemonic: For "important" – think "import + ant" – imagine an ant carrying something important.
  • Bangla Trick: Connect English words with similar-sounding Bangla words.
  • Visualization: Picture the word in action. For "achieve", imagine someone winning a trophy.
  • Group by Theme: Learn words in groups – emotions, actions, places, time, etc.
  • Spaced Repetition: Review words regularly using flashcards. Learn 20 new words daily and review yesterday's words.
💡 Tip: Use the word in a sentence immediately after learning it.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How many important English words do I need to know for BCS? ▾
For BCS Preliminary, you should know at least 2,000-3,000 important English words. This list of 1000 important words is a great starting point. For Bank Jobs and IELTS, aim for 3,000-5,000 words.
What is the best way to memorize important English words? ▾
The best way is to learn 20-30 words per day, use them in sentences, review regularly with spaced repetition, and practice with mock tests. Our Ovidhan app has built-in flashcards and spaced repetition for effective learning.
Are these 1000 words useful for IELTS preparation? ▾
Yes. These 1000 important words cover the core vocabulary needed for IELTS Speaking and Writing tasks. For higher bands (7+), you should also learn academic and less common words.
How long does it take to learn 1000 English words? ▾
If you learn 20 words per day, it will take 50 days (about 2 months). If you learn 30 words per day, it will take about 33 days. Consistency is more important than speed.
What is the difference between 'important' and 'necessary'? ▾
Important means having great significance or value. Necessary means something that is required or needed. Both words are frequently used in BCS and IELTS exams.
