📖 1. Introduction

Linking Words (also called connectors or transition words) are words or phrases that connect ideas, sentences, and paragraphs together.[reference:0] They help create coherence and logical flow in your writing and speaking. (Linking Words বা সংযোজক শব্দ হলো এমন শব্দ বা বাক্যাংশ যা বাক্য ও অনুচ্ছেদের মধ্যে সম্পর্ক স্থাপন করে।[reference:1])

For Bangladeshi students, linking words are essential for BCS, IELTS, HSC, and Bank Jobs. They are tested in sentence connectors, essay writing, and error correction sections. (বাংলাদেশী শিক্ষার্থীদের জন্য Linking Words অত্যন্ত গুরুত্বপূর্ণ।[reference:2])

💡 Remember This

Linking Words = Connectors = Transition Words = Words that connect ideas

বাংলা: Linking Words = সংযোজক শব্দ = বাক্য সংযোগকারী

📚 2. Definition & Core Concept

Linking words are used to join ideas together and give paragraphs coherence.[reference:3] They perform different functions and are placed at the beginning of a sentence or between clauses.[reference:4]

  • Word to word: boys and girls
  • Phrase to phrase: in the morning or in the evening
  • Clause to clause: I stayed home because it was raining
  • Sentence to sentence: I studied hard. However, I failed.
👨‍🏫 Teacher Tip

Linking words are the glue of English. Without them, your writing sounds choppy and disconnected.

➕ 3. Category 1: Addition – Adding Information

These linking words add information to what has already been said.[reference:5] (এই সংযোজক শব্দগুলো পূর্ববর্তী তথ্যের সাথে যোগ করে।)

Linking WordBangla MeaningExample
andএবংI like tea and coffee.[reference:6]
alsoএছাড়াওShe is smart. Also, she is kind.[reference:7]
moreoverআরওThe food is delicious. Moreover, it's affordable.[reference:8]
furthermoreআরও বেশিThe hotel is cheap. Furthermore, it's clean.[reference:9]
in additionএর পাশাপাশিIn addition, we need more time.[reference:10]
as well asসাথেHe speaks English as well as Bangla.[reference:11]
besidesএ ছাড়াBesides, I have work to do.[reference:12]
📝 "Bangladesh has beautiful beaches. Moreover, it has the world's largest mangrove forest."
📝 "বাংলাদেশে সুন্দর সৈকত আছে। আরও, এখানে বিশ্বের বৃহত্তম ম্যানগ্রোভ বন আছে।"

⚖️ 4. Category 2: Contrast – Showing Difference

These linking words show contrast or opposition between two ideas.[reference:13] (এই সংযোজক শব্দগুলো দুটি ধারণার মধ্যে বিরোধ বা পার্থক্য দেখায়।)

Linking WordBangla MeaningExample
butকিন্তুI like tea, but I don't like coffee.[reference:14]
howeverতবেI studied hard. However, I failed.[reference:15]
althoughযদিওAlthough he is poor, he is honest.[reference:16]
thoughযদিওHe is rich, though he is not happy.[reference:17]
whereasযখনHe is tall, whereas his brother is short.[reference:18]
on the other handঅন্যদিকেOn the other hand, she is very shy.[reference:19]
neverthelessতবুওIt was raining. Nevertheless, we went out.[reference:20]
📝 "Although Bangladesh is a small country, it has a large population."
📝 "যদিও বাংলাদেশ একটি ছোট দেশ, এর জনসংখ্যা অনেক বেশি।"
⚠️ Important Distinction

However = Used in a new sentence (I studied hard. However, I failed.)
Although = Used in the same sentence (Although I studied hard, I failed.)

⚡ 5. Category 3: Cause & Effect – Reason and Result

These linking words show reason or result of an action.[reference:21] (এই সংযোজক শব্দগুলো কোনো কাজের কারণ বা ফলাফল দেখায়।)

Linking WordBangla MeaningExample
becauseকারণI stayed home because it was raining.[reference:22]
soঅতএবIt was raining, so I stayed home.[reference:23]
thereforeঅতএবHe worked hard. Therefore, he succeeded.[reference:24]
sinceযেহেতুSince you are here, let's start.[reference:25]
asকারণAs it was late, we went home.[reference:26]
due toকারণেThe match was cancelled due to rain.
consequentlyফলেHe missed the bus. Consequently, he was late.
📝 "Because of the heavy rain, the match was cancelled."
📝 "কারণে প্রবল বৃষ্টির, ম্যাচটি বাতিল করা হয়েছিল।"
🧠 Memory Trick

Because + Clause (subject + verb): "because it was raining"
Because of + Noun: "because of the rain"

🔢 6. Category 4: Sequence – Order and Time

These linking words show the order of events or ideas.[reference:27] (এই সংযোজক শব্দগুলো ঘটনা বা ধারণার ক্রম দেখায়।)

Linking WordBangla MeaningExample
first / firstlyপ্রথমতFirstly, we need to plan.[reference:28]
secondlyদ্বিতীয়তSecondly, we need resources.[reference:29]
thenতারপরWe ate dinner, then we watched TV.
nextপরবর্তীNext, we need to review.[reference:30]
finallyশেষ পর্যন্তFinally, we presented the project.
after thatতার পরেWe went shopping. After that, we had lunch.
in the endশেষেIn the end, we succeeded.
📝 "Firstly, gather all materials. Secondly, start writing. Finally, edit and submit."
📝 "প্রথমত, সব উপকরণ সংগ্রহ করুন। দ্বিতীয়ত, লেখা শুরু করুন। শেষ পর্যন্ত, সম্পাদনা করে জমা দিন।"

📌 7. Category 5: Example – Giving Examples

These linking words introduce examples to support your ideas.[reference:31] (এই সংযোজক শব্দগুলো ধারণা সমর্থনের জন্য উদাহরণ দেয়।)

Linking WordBangla MeaningExample
for exampleউদাহরণস্বরূপMany countries, for example, Japan, are small.
for instanceউদাহরণস্বরূপFor instance, Bangladesh is a riverine country.
such asযেমনI like fruits such as mangoes and bananas.
likeযেমনCountries like Bangladesh need development.
📝 "Many Bangladeshi dishes, such as biryani and hilsa, are world-famous."
📝 "অনেক বাংলাদেশি খাবার, যেমন বিরিয়ানি ও ইলিশ, বিশ্ববিখ্যাত।"

🔀 8. Category 6: Condition – If and Unless

These linking words show condition or requirement for something to happen. (এই সংযোজক শব্দগুলো কোনো কিছুর জন্য শর্ত দেখায়।)

Linking WordBangla MeaningExample
ifযদিIf you study, you will pass.
unlessযদি নাYou will fail unless you study.
provided thatশর্তে যেYou can go provided that you finish work.
as long asযতক্ষণAs long as you try, you will succeed.
📝 "Unless you practice daily, you won't improve your English."
📝 "যদি না আপনি প্রতিদিন অনুশীলন করেন, আপনার ইংরেজি উন্নতি হবে না।"

❌ 9. Common Mistakes – Bangladeshi Students

❌ "Although he is rich, but he is not happy."
✅ "Although he is rich, he is not happy." (No "but" needed)

📖 Don't use 'but' with 'although' – they mean the same thing.

❌ "I studied hard. Although, I failed."
✅ "I studied hard. However, I failed."

📖 'Although' can't start a new sentence. Use 'However' instead.

❌ "Because of it was raining, I stayed home."
✅ "Because it was raining, I stayed home." or "Because of the rain, I stayed home."

📖 'Because of' + noun | 'Because' + clause (subject + verb)

❌ "I like tea, however I don't like coffee."
✅ "I like tea. However, I don't like coffee."

📖 'However' usually starts a new sentence with a comma.

🔄 10. Common Bangla Translation Errors

❌ "যদিও সে ধনী কিন্তু সে খুশি নয়।"
❌ "Although he is rich, but he is not happy."
✅ "Although he is rich, he is not happy."

📖 বাংলায় 'যদিও...কিন্তু' ব্যবহার করা হয়, কিন্তু ইংরেজিতে 'although' এবং 'but' একসাথে ব্যবহার করা যায় না।

❌ "বৃষ্টির কারণে আমি বাড়িতে থেকেছি।"
❌ "Because of it was raining, I stayed home."
✅ "Because it was raining, I stayed home." or "Because of the rain, I stayed home."

📖 'Because of' + noun, 'Because' + clause.

📧 Email Writing

🚫 13. Common Traps to Avoid

  • Don't overuse linking words. Use them naturally – 2-3 per paragraph is enough.
  • Don't use 'although' and 'but' together. Choose one.
  • Don't use 'however' as a conjunction. It starts a new sentence.
  • Don't confuse 'because' and 'because of'. 'Because' + clause, 'Because of' + noun.
❌ "Although he is poor, but he is honest."
✅ "Although he is poor, he is honest." or "He is poor, but he is honest."

👨‍🏫 15. Teacher Tips

💡 Tip 1 – 'Although' vs 'But'

Never use 'but' with 'although'. They do the same job. Choose one.

💡 Tip 2 – 'However' Rules

'However' starts a new sentence or follows a semicolon. It needs a comma after it.

💡 Tip 3 – 'Because' vs 'Because of'

Because + clause (subject + verb) | Because of + noun phrase

💡 Tip 4 – IELTS Coherence

In IELTS Writing, using a variety of linking words directly improves your Coherence and Cohesion score.

🇧🇩 Bangla Learner's Corner

🔥 Why Bangla speakers make these mistakes:

In Bengali, we often use double connectors like "যদিও...কিন্তু" (although...but). In English, we cannot use two connectors together. This is the most common mistake Bangladeshi students make.

🧠 Easy Bangla Memory Trick (টেকনিক):

"ইংরেজিতে এক বাক্যে একটি মাত্র সংযোজক ব্যবহার করুন।"
If you use 'although', don't use 'but'. If you use 'because', don't use 'so'.

🎯 Common BCS/IELTS Exam Trap:

BCS loves the "Although...but" trap. Remember: Never use 'but' with 'although'.

🇧🇩 Real-life Bangladesh Example:

❌ "Although Bangladesh is small, but it has a large population."
✅ "Although Bangladesh is small, it has a large population."
(এখানে 'although' এবং 'but' একসাথে ব্যবহার করা ভুল।)

❓ 20. Frequently Asked Questions

What are Linking Words in English Grammar? ▾
Linking Words (also called connectors or transition words) are words or phrases that connect ideas, sentences, and paragraphs together. They help create coherence and logical flow in writing and speaking.[reference:34]
Why are Linking Words important for BCS and IELTS? ▾
Linking Words are crucial for BCS, IELTS, and Bank Jobs because they test your ability to write coherent essays, complete sentence connectors, and understand logical relationships between ideas. In IELTS, they directly affect your Coherence and Cohesion score.[reference:35]
What are the main types of Linking Words? ▾
The main types are: Addition (and, also, moreover), Contrast (but, however, although), Cause and Effect (because, so, therefore), Sequence (first, then, finally), and Example (for example, such as).[reference:36]
What is the difference between 'because' and 'because of'? ▾
'Because' is a conjunction followed by a clause (subject + verb). Example: 'I stayed home because it was raining.' 'Because of' is a preposition followed by a noun phrase. Example: 'I stayed home because of the rain.'
What is the difference between 'however' and 'although'? ▾
'However' is used to introduce a contrasting statement in a new sentence. 'Although' is used to introduce a contrasting clause within the same sentence. Example: 'I studied hard. However, I failed.' vs 'Although I studied hard, I failed.'[reference:37]
What are cohesive devices in English? ▾
Cohesive devices are words or phrases that link sentences and paragraphs together to create a coherent text. They include linking words, pronouns, and conjunctions that help the reader follow the flow of ideas.[reference:38]
How many linking words should I use in an essay? ▾
Use linking words naturally to connect ideas. Overusing them can make your writing sound forced. Aim for 2-3 linking words per paragraph to maintain a natural flow.
What are sentence connectors in Bangla? ▾
Sentence connectors (বাক্য সংযোজক) are linking words that connect sentences together. Examples: 'তাছাড়া' (furthermore), 'অতএব' (therefore), 'তবে' (however), 'উদাহরণস্বরূপ' (for example).[reference:39]
Is 'and' a linking word? ▾
Yes, 'and' is one of the most common linking words. It is used to add information or connect similar ideas together.[reference:40]

📝 21. Summary

Linking Words are essential for coherent writing and speaking. Here are the key takeaways:

  • Addition: and, also, moreover, furthermore, in addition
  • Contrast: but, however, although, though, whereas, on the other hand
  • Cause & Effect: because, so, therefore, since, as, due to
  • Sequence: first, secondly, then, next, finally
  • Example: for example, for instance, such as, like
  • Condition: if, unless, provided that, as long as

📋 Quick Revision

Addition and, also, moreover, furthermore
Contrast but, however, although, whereas
Cause/Effect because, so, therefore, since
Sequence first, secondly, then, finally
Example for example, such as, like
Condition if, unless, provided that
💡 Golden Rule

"Never use two connectors together. Although...but and Because...so are common mistakes. Choose one!"
