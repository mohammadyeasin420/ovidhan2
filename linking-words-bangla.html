Linking Words in English Grammar with Bangla Meaning

📘 BCS 📘 IELTS 📘 HSC 📘 SSC 🎯 CEFR: B1–B2 | 50+ MCQ Quiz

English: Master Linking Words (Connectors) – the key to coherent writing and speaking.

বাংলা: Linking Words (সংযোজক শব্দ) সম্পূর্ণ গাইড। ৬টি ক্যাটেগরি, ৫০+ উদাহরণ, সাধারণ ভুল ও সংশোধন, এবং ৫০টি ইন্টারেক্টিভ প্রশ্ন – সবকিছু একসাথে।

🔍 What are Linking Words?

Linking Words (also called connectors or transition words) are words or phrases that connect ideas, sentences, and paragraphs together. They help create coherence and logical flow in your writing and speaking.

For Bangladeshi students, linking words are essential for BCS, IELTS, HSC, and Bank Jobs. They are tested in sentence connectors, essay writing, and error correction sections.

💡 Remember: Linking Words = Connectors = Transition Words = Words that connect ideas

📌 ৬টি ক্যাটেগরি – Linking Words এর প্রকারভেদ

1. Addition – যোগ (Adding Information)

এই সংযোজক শব্দগুলো পূর্ববর্তী তথ্যের সাথে যোগ করে।

Linking WordBangla MeaningExample
andএবংI like tea and coffee.
alsoএছাড়াওShe is smart. Also, she is kind.
moreoverআরওThe food is delicious. Moreover, it's affordable.
furthermoreআরও বেশিThe hotel is cheap. Furthermore, it's clean.
in additionএর পাশাপাশিIn addition, we need more time.
as well asসাথেHe speaks English as well as Bangla.
besidesএ ছাড়াBesides, I have work to do.

Example: "Bangladesh has beautiful beaches. Moreover, it has the world's largest mangrove forest."

"বাংলাদেশে সুন্দর সৈকত আছে। আরও, এখানে বিশ্বের বৃহত্তম ম্যানগ্রোভ বন আছে।"

2. Contrast – বিরোধ (Showing Difference)

এই সংযোজক শব্দগুলো দুটি ধারণার মধ্যে বিরোধ বা পার্থক্য দেখায়।

Linking WordBangla MeaningExample
butকিন্তুI like tea, but I don't like coffee.
howeverতবেI studied hard. However, I failed.
althoughযদিওAlthough he is poor, he is honest.
thoughযদিওHe is rich, though he is not happy.
whereasযখনHe is tall, whereas his brother is short.
on the other handঅন্যদিকেOn the other hand, she is very shy.
neverthelessতবুওIt was raining. Nevertheless, we went out.

Example: "Although Bangladesh is a small country, it has a large population."

"যদিও বাংলাদেশ একটি ছোট দেশ, এর জনসংখ্যা অনেক বেশি।"

⚠️ Important: However starts a new sentence. Although stays in the same sentence.

3. Cause & Effect – কারণ ও ফলাফল

এই সংযোজক শব্দগুলো কোনো কাজের কারণ বা ফলাফল দেখায়।

Linking WordBangla MeaningExample
becauseকারণI stayed home because it was raining.
soঅতএবIt was raining, so I stayed home.
thereforeঅতএবHe worked hard. Therefore, he succeeded.
sinceযেহেতুSince you are here, let's start.
asকারণAs it was late, we went home.
due toকারণেThe match was cancelled due to rain.
consequentlyফলেHe missed the bus. Consequently, he was late.

Example: "Because of the heavy rain, the match was cancelled."

"কারণে প্রবল বৃষ্টির, ম্যাচটি বাতিল করা হয়েছিল।"

🧠 Memory Trick: Because + Clause (subject + verb) | Because of + Noun

4. Sequence – ক্রম (Order and Time)

এই সংযোজক শব্দগুলো ঘটনা বা ধারণার ক্রম দেখায়।

Linking WordBangla MeaningExample
first / firstlyপ্রথমতFirstly, we need to plan.
secondlyদ্বিতীয়তSecondly, we need resources.
thenতারপরWe ate dinner, then we watched TV.
nextপরবর্তীNext, we need to review.
finallyশেষ পর্যন্তFinally, we presented the project.
after thatতার পরেWe went shopping. After that, we had lunch.
in the endশেষেIn the end, we succeeded.

Example: "Firstly, gather all materials. Secondly, start writing. Finally, edit and submit."

"প্রথমত, সব উপকরণ সংগ্রহ করুন। দ্বিতীয়ত, লেখা শুরু করুন। শেষ পর্যন্ত, সম্পাদনা করে জমা দিন।"

5. Example – উদাহরণ (Giving Examples)

এই সংযোজক শব্দগুলো ধারণা সমর্থনের জন্য উদাহরণ দেয়।

Linking WordBangla MeaningExample
for exampleউদাহরণস্বরূপMany countries, for example, Japan, are small.
for instanceউদাহরণস্বরূপFor instance, Bangladesh is a riverine country.
such asযেমনI like fruits such as mangoes and bananas.
likeযেমনCountries like Bangladesh need development.

Example: "Many Bangladeshi dishes, such as biryani and hilsa, are world-famous."

"অনেক বাংলাদেশি খাবার, যেমন বিরিয়ানি ও ইলিশ, বিশ্ববিখ্যাত।"

6. Condition – শর্ত (If and Unless)

এই সংযোজক শব্দগুলো কোনো কিছুর জন্য শর্ত দেখায়।

Linking WordBangla MeaningExample
ifযদিIf you study, you will pass.
unlessযদি নাYou will fail unless you study.
provided thatশর্তে যেYou can go provided that you finish work.
as long asযতক্ষণAs long as you try, you will succeed.

Example: "Unless you practice daily, you won't improve your English."

"যদি না আপনি প্রতিদিন অনুশীলন করেন, আপনার ইংরেজি উন্নতি হবে না।"

⚠️ Common Mistakes – Bangladeshi Students

❌ ভুল: Although he is rich, but he is not happy.

✅ শুদ্ধ: Although he is rich, he is not happy.

ব্যাখ্যা: 'Although' এবং 'but' একসাথে ব্যবহার করা যায় না।

❌ ভুল: I studied hard. Although, I failed.

✅ শুদ্ধ: I studied hard. However, I failed.

ব্যাখ্যা: 'Although' নতুন বাক্য শুরু করতে পারে না। 'However' ব্যবহার করুন।

❌ ভুল: Because of it was raining, I stayed home.

✅ শুদ্ধ: Because it was raining, I stayed home. অথবা Because of the rain, I stayed home.

ব্যাখ্যা: 'Because of' + noun, 'Because' + clause.

❌ ভুল: I like tea, however I don't like coffee.

✅ শুদ্ধ: I like tea. However, I don't like coffee.

ব্যাখ্যা: 'However' সাধারণত নতুন বাক্য শুরু করে এবং কমা দিয়ে অনুসরণ করে।

❌ ভুল: He is poor, so he is honest.

✅ শুদ্ধ: He is poor, but he is honest. অথবা Although he is poor, he is honest.

ব্যাখ্যা: 'So' ফলাফল বোঝায়, 'but' বা 'although' বিরোধ বোঝায়।

🔄 Common Bangla Translation Errors

❌ ভুল (Bangla): "যদিও সে ধনী কিন্তু সে খুশি নয়।"

❌ ভুল (English): "Although he is rich, but he is not happy."

✅ শুদ্ধ: "Although he is rich, he is not happy."

📖 বাংলায় 'যদিও...কিন্তু' ব্যবহার করা হয়, কিন্তু ইংরেজিতে 'although' এবং 'but' একসাথে ব্যবহার করা যায় না।

❌ ভুল (Bangla): "বৃষ্টির কারণে আমি বাড়িতে থেকেছি।"

❌ ভুল (English): "Because of it was raining, I stayed home."

✅ শুদ্ধ: "Because it was raining, I stayed home." অথবা "Because of the rain, I stayed home."

📖 'Because of' + noun, 'Because' + clause.

📧 Email Writing

"যেহেতু আপনি অনুরোধ করেছিলেন, আমি নথিটি সংযুক্ত করেছি। আরও বেশি, আমি একটি সারসংক্ষেপ অন্তর্ভুক্ত করেছি। দয়া করে এটি পর্যালোচনা করুন এবং আপনার মতামত জানান।"

🚫 Common Traps to Avoid

  • Don't overuse linking words. Use them naturally – 2-3 per paragraph is enough.
  • Don't use 'although' and 'but' together. Choose one.
  • Don't use 'however' as a conjunction. It starts a new sentence.
  • Don't confuse 'because' and 'because of'. 'Because' + clause, 'Because of' + noun.
  • Don't start a sentence with 'because' in formal writing unless it's a full clause.
❌ "Although he is poor, but he is honest."
✅ "Although he is poor, he is honest." or "He is poor, but he is honest."

👨‍🏫 Teacher Tips

💡 Tip 1 – 'Although' vs 'But'

Never use 'but' with 'although'. They do the same job. Choose one.

💡 Tip 2 – 'However' Rules

'However' starts a new sentence or follows a semicolon. It needs a comma after it.

💡 Tip 3 – 'Because' vs 'Because of'

Because + clause (subject + verb) | Because of + noun phrase

💡 Tip 4 – IELTS Coherence

In IELTS Writing, using a variety of linking words directly improves your Coherence and Cohesion score.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What are Linking Words in English Grammar? ▾
Linking Words (also called connectors or transition words) are words or phrases that connect ideas, sentences, and paragraphs together. They help create coherence and logical flow in writing and speaking.
Why are Linking Words important for BCS and IELTS? ▾
Linking Words are crucial for BCS, IELTS, and Bank Jobs because they test your ability to write coherent essays, complete sentence connectors, and understand logical relationships between ideas. In IELTS, they directly affect your Coherence and Cohesion score.
What are the main types of Linking Words? ▾
The main types are: Addition (and, also, moreover), Contrast (but, however, although), Cause and Effect (because, so, therefore), Sequence (first, then, finally), and Example (for example, such as).
What is the difference between 'because' and 'because of'? ▾
'Because' is a conjunction followed by a clause (subject + verb). Example: 'I stayed home because it was raining.' 'Because of' is a preposition followed by a noun phrase. Example: 'I stayed home because of the rain.'
What is the difference between 'however' and 'although'? ▾
'However' is used to introduce a contrasting statement in a new sentence. 'Although' is used to introduce a contrasting clause within the same sentence. Example: 'I studied hard. However, I failed.' vs 'Although I studied hard, I failed.'

📝 Summary

Linking Words are essential for coherent writing and speaking. Here are the key takeaways:

  • Addition: and, also, moreover, furthermore, in addition
  • Contrast: but, however, although, though, whereas, on the other hand
  • Cause & Effect: because, so, therefore, since, as, due to
  • Sequence: first, secondly, then, next, finally
  • Example: for example, for instance, such as, like
  • Condition: if, unless, provided that, as long as
💡 Golden Rule: "Never use two connectors together. Although...but and Because...so are common mistakes. Choose one!"
