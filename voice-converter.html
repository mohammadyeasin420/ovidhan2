📚 Active vs Passive Voice

Understanding active and passive voice is essential for BCS, IELTS, Bank Jobs, HSC, and university admission. (Active এবং Passive Voice বোঝা BCS, IELTS, ব্যাংক জব, HSC এবং বিশ্ববিদ্যালয় ভর্তির জন্য অত্যন্ত গুরুত্বপূর্ণ।)

📌 Active Voice

In active voice, the subject performs the action. (Active voice-এ উদ্দেশ্য কাজটি সম্পাদন করে।)

📝 Subject + Verb + Object
উদ্দেশ্য + ক্রিয়া + কর্ম
Example: "The cat ate the fish."
উদাহরণ: "বিড়াল মাছটি খেয়েছে।"

📌 Passive Voice

In passive voice, the subject receives the action. (Passive voice-এ উদ্দেশ্য কাজটি গ্রহণ করে।)

📝 Object + be + V3 + by + Subject
কর্ম + be + V3 + by + উদ্দেশ্য
Example: "The fish was eaten by the cat."
উদাহরণ: "মাছটি বিড়াল দ্বারা খাওয়া হয়েছে।"

📌 Voice Change Rules

Present Continuousam/is/are + V-ingam/is/are + being + V3

Past Continuouswas/were + V-ingwas/were + being + V3
Past Perfecthad + V3had + been + V3
Future Indefinitewill + V1will + be + V3

❌ Common Mistakes

❌ "The fish was ate by the cat."
✅ "The fish was eaten by the cat."

📖 Explanation: After "was/were", use the past participle (V3) – "eaten", not "ate". Learn more →

❌ "A letter is write by her."
✅ "A letter is written by her."

📖 Explanation: Use the past participle (V3) – "written", not "write". Learn more →
