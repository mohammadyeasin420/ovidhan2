📚 Understanding English Verb Forms

Every English verb has five forms. Understanding them is essential for BCS, IELTS, Bank Jobs, HSC, and university admission. (প্রত্যেকটি ইংরেজি ক্রিয়াপদের পাঁচটি রূপ রয়েছে। এগুলো বোঝা অত্যন্ত গুরুত্বপূর্ণ।)

📌 The Five Verb Forms

Form Example Bangla Usage
Base (V1) go যাওয়া Present tense (I, You, We, They)
Past (V2) went গিয়েছিল Past tense
Past Participle (V3) gone গিয়েছে Perfect tenses, Passive voice
Present Participle (V4) going যাচ্ছে Continuous tenses
3rd Person Singular (V5) goes যায় Present tense (He, She, It)

📌 Regular vs Irregular Verbs

Regular Verbs

Regular verbs form their past tense and past participle by adding -ed. (নিয়মিত ক্রিয়াপদগুলি past tense এবং past participle-এ -ed যোগ করে গঠন করে।)

📝 walk → walked → walked
হাঁটা → হাঁটল → হেঁটেছে
📝 play → played → played
খেলা → খেলল → খেলেছে
📝 work → worked → worked
কাজ করা → কাজ করল → কাজ করেছে

Irregular Verbs

Irregular verbs change form completely and must be memorized. (অনিয়মিত ক্রিয়াপদগুলি সম্পূর্ণভাবে পরিবর্তিত হয় এবং মুখস্থ করতে হয়।)

📝 go → went → gone
যাওয়া → গিয়েছিল → গিয়েছে
📝 write → wrote → written
লেখা → লিখেছিল → লিখেছে
📝 eat → ate → eaten
খাওয়া → খেয়েছিল → খেয়েছে

📌 Common Verb Form Mistakes

❌ "He go to school."
✅ "He goes to school."

📖 Explanation: Third person singular (he/she/it) needs -s/-es.

❌ "I have went there."
✅ "I have gone there."

📖 Explanation: After "have" use the past participle (V3), not past (V2).

❌ "She was wrote a letter."
✅ "She wrote a letter." / "She was writing a letter."

📖 Explanation: Use V2 for past tense, not V3. Use V4 for continuous tense.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What are the five verb forms? ▾
The five verb forms are: Base Form (V1), Past Form (V2), Past Participle (V3), Present Participle (V4), and 3rd Person Singular (V5). For example: go → went → gone → going → goes.
What is the difference between regular and irregular verbs? ▾
Regular verbs form their past tense by adding -ed (e.g., walk → walked). Irregular verbs change form completely (e.g., go → went → gone). Our Verb Forms tool shows both types with examples.
Why is learning verb forms important for BCS and IELTS? ▾
Verb forms are essential for BCS, IELTS, and Bank Job exams. Questions appear in error correction, sentence completion, and transformation sections. Mastering verb forms helps you score higher.
How can I remember irregular verb forms? ▾
Group irregular verbs by patterns. For example: sing-sang-sung, ring-rang-rung. Practice regularly and use our Verb Forms tool to search any verb instantly.
