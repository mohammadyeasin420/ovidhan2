📚 6 Plural Rules in English (With Examples)

Understanding plural rules is essential for BCS, IELTS, Bank Jobs, HSC, and everyday communication. (বহুবচন নিয়ম বোঝা BCS, IELTS, ব্যাংক জব, HSC এবং দৈনন্দিন যোগাযোগের জন্য অত্যন্ত গুরুত্বপূর্ণ।) Below are the 6 main rules with examples and Bangla translations.

1️⃣ Add '-s' (Regular Nouns)

Most nouns form their plural by simply adding -s.

📝 book → books, pen → pens, car → cars
বই → বইগুলি, কলম → কলমগুলি, গাড়ি → গাড়িগুলি

2️⃣ Add '-es' (Nouns Ending in s, sh, ch, x, z)

Nouns ending in s, sh, ch, x, or z add -es.

📝 bus → buses, box → boxes, church → churches
বাস → বাসগুলি, বাক্স → বাক্সগুলি, গির্জা → গির্জাগুলি

3️⃣ Change 'y' to 'ies' (Consonant + y)

Nouns ending in consonant + y change the y to i and add -es.

📝 city → cities, baby → babies, country → countries
শহর → শহরগুলি, শিশু → শিশুরা, দেশ → দেশগুলি

4️⃣ Vowel + 'y' → Add '-s'

Nouns ending in vowel + y simply add -s.

📝 boy → boys, toy → toys, day → days
ছেলে → ছেলেরা, খেলনা → খেলনাগুলি, দিন → দিনগুলি

5️⃣ Change 'f/fe' to 'ves'

Many nouns ending in f or fe change to ves.

📝 knife → knives, leaf → leaves, wife → wives
ছুরি → ছুরিগুলি, পাতা → পাতাগুলি, স্ত্রী → স্ত্রীরা

⚠️ Exceptions: roof → roofs, safe → safes (add -s).

6️⃣ Irregular Plurals

Some nouns have completely irregular plural forms that must be memorized.

📝 child → children, man → men, woman → women, mouse → mice
শিশু → শিশুরা, পুরুষ → পুরুষরা, মহিলা → মহিলারা, ইঁদুর → ইঁদুরগুলি

⚠️ Some nouns are the same in singular and plural: sheep, deer, fish.

📌 Common Mistakes

❌ "I have two childs."
✅ "I have two children."

📖 Explanation: "Child" is irregular – its plural is "children".

❌ "The citys are beautiful."
✅ "The cities are beautiful."

📖 Explanation: "City" ends in consonant + y → change y to i and add es.

❌ "She bought two knife."
✅ "She bought two knives."

📖 Explanation: "Knife" ends in fe → change to ves.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the plural of child? ▾
The plural of child is children. It's an irregular plural noun and does not follow standard rules.
What is the plural of woman? ▾
The plural of woman is women. It's an irregular plural that changes the vowel sound.
What is the plural of sheep? ▾
The plural of sheep is sheep. It is an irregular noun that remains the same in both singular and plural forms.
What is the plural of city? ▾
The plural of city is cities. This follows the rule: nouns ending in consonant + 'y' change 'y' to 'i' and add 'es'.
How many plural rules are there in English? ▾
There are 6 main plural rules in English: 1) Add -s, 2) Add -es, 3) Change y to ies, 4) Change f/fe to ves, 5) Irregular plurals, and 6) Nouns with the same form. Our Plural Maker tool covers all of them with examples.
What is the plural of fish? ▾
The plural of fish is fish (or fishes when referring to different species). It is an irregular noun.
What is the plural of child in Bangla? ▾
The plural of child is children. In Bangla, it translates to 'শিশুরা' (shishura).
