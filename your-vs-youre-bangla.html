📖 1. Introduction

Your and You're are two of the most commonly confused words in English. They sound exactly the same but have completely different meanings and uses. (Your এবং You're ইংরেজির সবচেয়ে সাধারণ দ্ব্যর্থক শব্দগুলির মধ্যে দুটি। এগুলি সম্পূর্ণ একই রকম শোনায় কিন্তু সম্পূর্ণ ভিন্ন অর্থ ও ব্যবহার রয়েছে।)

The rule is simple: Your is a possessive adjective meaning belonging to you, and You're is a contraction of "you are". This guide will help you master this pair with clear explanations, examples, and memory tricks. (নিয়মটি সহজ: Your একটি অধিকারসূচক বিশেষণ যার অর্থ তোমার/আপনার, এবং You're হলো "you are" এর সংক্ষিপ্ত রূপ।)

💡 Remember This

Your = belongs to you (possession)

You're = you are (contraction)

📝 "Is this your book?" (Possession – তোমার বই?)
📝 "এটি কি তোমার বই?"
📝 "You're very kind." (You are – তুমি খুব দয়ালু)
📝 "তুমি খুব দয়ালু।"

📚 2. Definition – Your vs You're

Your
(Possessive Adjective)
তোমার, আপনার
Belonging to you
You're
(Contraction)
তুমি ...আছ, আপনি ...আছেন
You are (shortened form)
👨‍🏫 The Golden Rule

Your = Possession (belongs to you)

You're = You are (contraction)

Your = অধিকার | You're = You are

🧠 4. Memory Trick – Never Forget Again

Use these simple tricks to remember the difference forever: (পার্থক্যটি চিরকাল মনে রাখতে এই সহজ কৌশলগুলি ব্যবহার করুন:)

💡 Memory Trick #1 – The Replacement Test

If you can replace it with "you are", use you're.

If you can't, use your.

💡 Memory Trick #2 – The Apostrophe Rule

You're has an apostrophe – it's a contraction of two words.

Your has no apostrophe – it's a possessive adjective.

💡 Memory Trick #3 – Bangla Connection

Your = তোমার (when you mean "belongs to you")

You're = তুমি (when you mean "you are")

📝 5. Examples with Bangla Translation

Here are examples using real Bangladeshi contexts: (বাংলাদেশী প্রসঙ্গ ব্যবহার করে উদাহরণ দেওয়া হলো:)

🔹 Your (Possession – তোমার/আপনার)

1. "Is this your book?"
"এটি কি তোমার বই?"
2. "I like your new bag."
"আমি তোমার নতুন ব্যাগ পছন্দ করি।"
3. "Where is your house?"
"তোমার বাড়ি কোথায়?"
4. "Please give me your phone number."
"অনুগ্রহ করে আপনার ফোন নম্বর দিন।"
5. "I love your cooking."
"আমি তোমার রান্না ভালোবাসি।"

🔸 You're (You are – তুমি/আপনি ...আছ)

1. "You're very kind."
"তুমি খুব দয়ালু।"
2. "You're going to Dhaka tomorrow?"
"তুমি আগামীকাল ঢাকায় যাচ্ছ?"
3. "You're my best friend."
"তুমি আমার সবচেয়ে ভালো বন্ধু।"
4. "You're welcome."
"তোমাকে স্বাগতম।" (আপনাকে ধন্যবাদ জানানোর উত্তর)
5. "You're doing a great job."
"তুমি দারুণ কাজ করছ।"

🔹 Side-by-Side Comparison

Your: "I appreciate your help."
"আমি তোমার সাহায্যের প্রশংসা করি।"
You're: "You're very helpful."
"তুমি খুব সহায়ক।"
💡 Remember

Your = Possession (your book, your house)

You're = You are (you're kind, you're going)

❌ 6. Common Mistakes Table

Here are the most common mistakes Bangladeshi students make with your and you're: (বাংলাদেশী শিক্ষার্থীরা your এবং you're নিয়ে সবচেয়ে বেশি যে ভুলগুলি করে:)

❌ Wrong✅ CorrectWhy?
Your welcome. You're welcome. "You're welcome" = you are welcome.
You're book. Your book. "Your" shows possession (belongs to you).
Your coming. You're coming. "You're coming" = you are coming.
I like you're shirt. I like your shirt. "Your" shows possession (the shirt belongs to you).
💡 Quick Test

If you can say "you are" in the sentence, use you're. If not, use your.

❓ 10. Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between your and you're? ▾
Your is a possessive adjective meaning belonging to you. You're is a contraction of 'you are'. For example: 'Is this your book?' (possession) and 'You're very kind' (you are).
How can I remember the difference between your and you're? ▾
Use the memory trick: If you can replace it with 'you are', use you're. Otherwise, use your. For example: 'You're (you are) coming' vs 'Your book'.
Can 'your' be used as a contraction? ▾
No, 'your' is never a contraction. It is always a possessive adjective. If you need to mean 'you are', you must write 'you're' with an apostrophe.
Is it 'your welcome' or 'you're welcome'? ▾
It is 'you're welcome' – meaning 'you are welcome'. Many people mistakenly write 'your welcome' because of the similar pronunciation.
Is your or you're more common in BCS exams? ▾
Both are frequently tested in BCS, IELTS, and Bank Job exams. The most common test is choosing between 'your' (possession) and 'you're' (you are) in error correction and sentence completion.

📝 11. Summary

Mastering the difference between Your and You're is essential for clear communication and exam success. Here are the key takeaways:

  • Your = Possessive Adjective – belonging to you (তোমার/আপনার)
  • You're = Contraction – you are (তুমি/আপনি ...আছ)
  • Memory Trick: Replace with "you are" – if it fits, use you're
  • Apostrophe: You're has an apostrophe (contraction), your does not
  • Exam Focus: In BCS, IELTS, and Bank Jobs, the possession vs contraction rule is most tested
