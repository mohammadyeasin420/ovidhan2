✍️ 1. Introduction – Why Writing Matters

Let me be honest with you. When I first started learning English, I could read and understand a lot. But when I tried to write, my sentences were messy. I didn't know how to structure paragraphs. I made the same mistakes over and over. I felt embarrassed when someone read my writing. (আমি যখন প্রথম ইংরেজি শিখতে শুরু করি, আমি অনেক কিছু পড়তে এবং বুঝতে পারতাম। কিন্তু যখন আমি লেখার চেষ্টা করতাম, আমার বাক্যগুলো এলোমেলো হত। আমি জানতাম না কীভাবে অনুচ্ছেদ গঠন করতে হয়। আমি বারবার একই ভুল করতাম। যখন কেউ আমার লেখা পড়ত, আমি বিব্রত বোধ করতাম।)

Writing is different from speaking. It requires structure, grammar, and clarity. You can't rely on body language or tone. Your words have to do all the work. But writing is also a skill you can learn — just like any other skill.

In this guide, I will teach you how to write English—from basic sentences to complete essays. You will learn sentence structure, paragraph writing, essay writing, letter writing, and common mistakes. For daily practice exercises, visit our Practice Hub.

💡 A Lesson I'll Never Forget

The day I started writing in English every day was the day I finally improved. I wrote simple sentences at first. Then paragraphs. Then essays. Writing is like a muscle — the more you use it, the stronger it gets.
