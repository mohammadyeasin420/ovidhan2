Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

Summary: অভিধান is a 100% offline app. We do not collect, store, or share your personal data. All your data stays on your device.

1. Introduction

অভিধান ("Ovidhan", "we", "our") is a 100% offline English to Bangla dictionary app developed by Mohammad Yeasin. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number, or address)
  • No account creation required
  • No cloud sync or server-side storage
  • No location data
  • All your favorites, progress, and scores stay on your device only

3. AdMob Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display non-intrusive advertisements. AdMob may collect anonymous data for ad personalization per Google's own privacy policy.

  • Publisher ID: pub-8638443498834829
  • You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings: Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • AdMob privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

4. App Permissions

  • Storage: To save favorites, bookmarks, and progress locally on your device
  • Notifications: Optional daily challenge and streak reminders (can be disabled)
  • Internet: Used only by AdMob for ads. The dictionary works fully offline.

5. Children's Privacy

অভিধান is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13.

6. Your Rights & Data Deletion

  • Go to Settings → Apps → অভিধান → Clear Data to delete everything
  • Uninstalling the app removes all associated data
  • No data is retained by us after uninstall

7. Third-Party Services

The only third-party service integrated is Google AdMob. No other analytics, crash reporting, or tracking services are used.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the date above. Continued use of the app means you accept the updated policy.
