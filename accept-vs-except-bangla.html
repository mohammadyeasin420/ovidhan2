📖 1. Introduction

Accept and Except are two of the most commonly confused word pairs in English. They look similar but have completely different meanings and uses. (Accept এবং Except ইংরেজির সবচেয়ে সাধারণ দ্ব্যর্থক শব্দ জোড়াগুলির মধ্যে একটি। এগুলি দেখতে একই রকম কিন্তু সম্পূর্ণ ভিন্ন অর্থ ও ব্যবহার রয়েছে।)

The rule is simple: Accept is a verb meaning to receive or agree to something, and Except is a preposition meaning excluding or apart from. (নিয়মটি সহজ: Accept একটি ক্রিয়া যার অর্থ গ্রহণ করা বা সম্মত হওয়া, এবং Except একটি পদবি যার অর্থ ছাড়া বা বাদে।)

💡 Remember This

Accept = Agree to take (both start with A)

Except = Exclude (both start with Ex)

📝 "I accept your apology." (Receive – গ্রহণ করা)
📝 "আমি আপনার ক্ষমা চাওয়া গ্রহণ করি।"
📝 "Everyone went except me." (Excluding – ছাড়া)
📝 "আমি ছাড়া সবাই গিয়েছিলাম।"

📚 2. Definition – Accept vs Except

Accept
(Verb)
গ্রহণ করা, সম্মত হওয়া
To receive or agree to something
Except
(Preposition)
ছাড়া, বাদে
Excluding or apart from
👨‍🏫 The Golden Rule

Accept = Verb (to receive) | Except = Preposition (excluding)

Accept = ক্রিয়া (গ্রহণ করা) | Except = পদবি (বাদে)

🧠 4. Memory Trick – Never Forget Again

Use these simple tricks to remember the difference forever: (পার্থক্যটি চিরকাল মনে রাখতে এই সহজ কৌশলগুলি ব্যবহার করুন:)

💡 Memory Trick #1 – The A & Ex Rule

Accept = Agree to take (both start with A)

Except = Exclude (both start with Ex)

💡 Memory Trick #2 – Bangla Connection

Accept = গ্রহণ করা – when you take something

Except = ছাড়া – when you exclude something

💡 Memory Trick #3 – The Exception Rule

Except is used to make an exception – both have "ex" at the beginning!

1. "Everyone went except me."

"সে ছাড়া সবাই পাস করেছে।"
5. "I eat everything except fish."
"মাছ ছাড়া আমি সবকিছু খাই।"

🔹 Side-by-Side Comparison

Accept: "I accept your invitation to the wedding."
"আমি বিয়ের আমন্ত্রণ গ্রহণ করি।"
Except: "Everyone came except Rahim."
"রহিম ছাড়া সবাই এসেছিল।"
💡 Remember

Accept = Agree (to receive) | Except = Exclude (to leave out)

🎯 7. Exam Corner – BCS, IELTS & HSC

Here are typical exam questions and their answers: (এখানে সাধারণ পরীক্ষার প্রশ্ন এবং তাদের উত্তর দেওয়া হলো:)

📘 BCS / Bank
"Everyone went ______ Rahim."
✅ Correct: except
Explanation: "Except" means excluding.
📘 BCS / Bank
"I ______ your apology."
✅ Correct: accept
Explanation: "Accept" is a verb meaning to receive.
🌍 IELTS
"All students passed the exam ______ John."
✅ Correct: except
Explanation: John is the only one who didn't pass.
🎒 HSC / SSC
"She ______ the job offer happily."
✅ Correct: accepted
Explanation: "Accepted" is the past tense of "accept."

In BCS and Bank Job exams, the most common test is: accept = verb (receive) and except = preposition (excluding). Remember the A & Ex rule!

❓ 10. Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between accept and except? ▾
Accept is a verb meaning to receive or agree to something. Except is a preposition meaning excluding or apart from. For example: 'I accept your apology' (receive) and 'Everyone went except me' (excluding).
How can I remember the difference between accept and except? ▾
Use the memory trick: Accept = Agree to take (both start with A). Except = Exclude (both start with Ex). Or remember: 'Accept' has an 'a' for 'acceptance', 'Except' has an 'e' for 'exclude'.
Can 'accept' be used as a preposition? ▾
No, 'accept' is always a verb. It cannot be used as a preposition. If you want to mean 'excluding', use 'except' as the preposition.
Can 'except' be used as a verb? ▾
Yes, 'except' can be used as a verb meaning 'to exclude' or 'to leave out'. For example: 'The new policy excepts small businesses.' This is a formal use.
Is accept or except more common in BCS exams? ▾
Both are frequently tested in BCS, IELTS, and Bank Job exams. The most common test is choosing between 'accept' (verb) and 'except' (preposition) in sentence completion and error correction.

📝 11. Summary

Mastering the difference between Accept and Except is essential for clear communication and exam success. Here are the key takeaways:

  • Accept = Verb – to receive or agree to something (গ্রহণ করা)
  • Except = Preposition – excluding or apart from (ছাড়া)
  • Memory Trick: Accept = Agree (A), Except = Exclude (Ex)
  • Bangla Connection: Accept = গ্রহণ করা | Except = ছাড়া
  • Exam Focus: In BCS, IELTS, and Bank Jobs, the verb/preposition rule is most tested
